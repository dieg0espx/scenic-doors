## 2. Requesting a Quote

The quote wizard walks you through a simple step-by-step process. How many steps you see depends on how much detail you want to provide.

### Step 1: Contact Information

The first screen asks for your contact details so we can reach you. Fill in every field — all are required unless marked optional.

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **First Name** | Your first name |
| **Last Name** | Your last name |
| **Email** | Your email address — this is where we'll send your quote and all updates |
| **Phone** | Your phone number |
| **ZIP Code** | Your 5-digit ZIP code (helps us determine your service area) |
| **Referral Code** | *(Optional)* If someone referred you, enter their code here. If you arrived via a referral link, this is pre-filled and hidden. |
| **Project Timeline** | Select the option that best matches your timing: "ASAP (0–30 days)," "1–3 months," "3–6 months," "6+ months / Planning," or "Just researching" |
| **What best describes you?** | Choose one: "Homeowner," "Contractor/Builder," "Architect/Designer," "Dealer/Reseller," or "Other" |
| **How did you hear about us?** | Choose one: "Google/Search," "Referral," "Social Media," "Event/Showroom," or "Other" |

When you're done, click **Continue** (on mobile) or **Continue to Product Selection** (on desktop).

> **Tip:** Double-check your email address — it's the primary way we'll communicate with you throughout the process.

---

### Step 2: Choosing Your Path

Next you'll see the question **"How can we help you today?"** with three options. Pick the one that best matches where you are in your project:

#### Option 1 — "Just browsing prices"
- **Best for:** Getting a quick feel for pricing with no commitment.
- **What happens:** You'll see estimated pricing for each product type, then submit your interest. A specialist will follow up.
- **Total steps:** 4

#### Option 2 — "I have a general idea"
- **Best for:** You know roughly what you want but don't have exact measurements yet.
- **What happens:** You'll select a product, answer a few preference questions, and we'll prepare a tailored estimate.
- **Total steps:** 6

#### Option 3 — "I know exactly what I want"
- **Best for:** You have specific dimensions, finish preferences, and are ready for a precise quote.
- **What happens:** You'll configure every detail — dimensions, panels, colors, glass, and hardware — and receive a detailed, itemized quote.
- **Total steps:** 6

---

### Browse Prices Path

If you chose **"Just browsing prices,"** you'll see product cards for all four door systems. Each card shows:

- Product name and image
- Per-square-foot rate
- Three key features
- A **"See How It Works"** button that opens an animation showing how the door operates
- A **"Select This Door"** button if you're interested

Select the products you're interested in, then click **Submit**. You'll see a confirmation page and a specialist will reach out to discuss pricing in more detail.

---

### General Idea Path

If you chose **"I have a general idea,"** here's what to expect:

#### Step 3: Select Your Product
Choose one of the four door systems. Each card shows the product image, per-square-foot rate, and key features. Click **"Select This Door"** to continue.

#### Step 4: General Preferences
Answer four quick questions:

1. **Approximate Opening Size** — Choose from: Small (Under 8'), Medium (8'–12'), Large (12'–16'), Extra Large (16'+), or "Not sure."
2. **Color Preference** — Choose from: Light (White/Ivory), Dark (Black/Bronze), Wood Tones, or "Not sure."
3. **Glass Preference** — Choose from: Standard (Clear glass), Energy Efficient (Low-E coating), Privacy/Tinted, or "Not sure."
4. **Project Notes** *(optional)* — A text box where you can describe your project, room type, timeline, or any special requirements.

Click **Continue to Summary** when you're done.

#### Step 5: Review & Submit
You'll see a summary of your selection and preferences, plus information about available services (delivery and professional installation). Review everything, then click **"Submit Estimate Request."**

### Full Quote Path

If you chose **"I know exactly what I want,"** you'll configure your door in full detail.

#### Step 3: Select Your Product
Same product selection screen as above — choose the door system you want.

#### Step 4: Full Configuration
This is where you dial in every specification. A mini progress bar at the top tracks your progress through four sections:

**Section 1 — Size & Configuration**

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **System Type** | *(Multi-Slide and Ultra Slim only)* Toggle between "Sliders and Multi-Slide Systems" or "Pocket Door System." For pocket systems, do not include the pocket area in your width — the system calculates it automatically. |
| **Width (inches)** | Enter the width of your opening in inches. Maximum depends on the product (e.g., 240″ for Multi-Slide, 292″ for Slide & Stack). |
| **Height (inches)** | Enter the height in inches. Maximum varies by product (e.g., 140″ for Multi-Slide, 120″ for Bi-Fold). |
| **Panel Count** | After entering width, you'll see available panel options. Each option shows the number of panels and the approximate per-panel width (e.g., "4 Panels — ≈ 40″ each"). |
| **Panel Layout** | After selecting panel count, choose how the panels are arranged — for example, All Left, All Right, or Split. Layout diagrams are displayed so you can see exactly how each option looks. A note reminds you: *"As viewed from outside the building."* |

A live door animation preview shows how your configured door will operate.

**Section 2 — Color Selection**

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Exterior Finish** | Choose from: Black, White, Bronze (paint), or Anodized Aluminum. Color swatches are shown. |
| **Two-Tone** | Toggle this on if you want a different interior color. |
| **Interior Finish** | *(Only if Two-Tone is on)* Choose from the same color options. |

**Section 3 — Glass Options**

For **Multi-Slide, Ultra Slim,** and **Awning** products, you'll see detailed glass cards:

| Glass Type | Description | Price |
|------------|-------------|-------|
| **Low-E3 Glass** | Low-emissivity coating for energy efficiency, UV protection, reduced fading. Dual-pane tempered. | Included |
| **Clear Glass** | Standard clear insulated glass. Maximum light transmission. Dual-pane tempered. | −$50 per panel |
| **Laminated Glass** | Safety glass with enhanced security, sound reduction. Impact glass. | +$75 per panel |

For **Bi-Fold** and **Slide & Stack** products, a simplified list with thumbnails is shown.

**Section 4 — Hardware Finish**

Choose the finish for handles and hardware: Black, White, or Silver (Bronze is available for Awning windows).

When all four sections are complete, click **"Save Configuration."** If anything is missing, you'll see an amber warning with a list of what still needs to be selected.

#### Adding Multiple Items
After saving your first item, you'll move to the summary screen. From there, you can click **"Add Another Item"** to configure additional doors or windows for the same quote.

## 3. Reviewing Your Quote

### Receiving the Quote Email
After you (or our team) submit a quote, you'll receive an email with the subject:

> **Your Quote QT-XXXX-XXX from Scenic Doors**

The email includes your quote details (door type, material, color, glass, size, and cost), delivery information, and a **link to your client portal** where you can view the full quote and take action.

### Viewing Your Quote
Click the link in the email to open your quote page. You'll see:

- Your **quote number** and status
- A **door animation** preview showing how your door operates
- A **specifications grid** listing every detail of your configuration
- **Delivery information** (if applicable)
- Any **notes** from our team
- Your **Grand Total** displayed prominently

### Accepting or Declining

At the bottom of the quote page, you have two options:

- **"Accept Quote"** (green button) — Submits your acceptance. Our team will review and send you next steps (contract signing). You'll see a confirmation message: *"Thank you for accepting the quote! Our team is reviewing your acceptance and you'll receive an email with the next steps shortly."*

- **"Decline"** (gray button) — Lets us know you've decided not to proceed. You'll see: *"Thank you for your time. If you change your mind or have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us."*

> **Note:** Accepting a quote does not commit you to payment yet. The next step is reviewing and signing the contract.

## 4. Your Client Portal

Once your quote is sent, you'll have access to your personal client portal. The portal link is included in your quote email and remains active throughout your project.

### Progress Bar

At the top of the portal, a **9-stage progress bar** shows exactly where your project stands:

| Stage | Label | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | **Quote** | Your quote has been sent |
| 2 | **Drawing Requested** | You've requested an approval drawing |
| 3 | **Approval** | Your approval drawing is ready for review |
| 4 | **Signed** | You've signed the approval drawing |
| 5 | **Deposit** | Waiting for your 50% advance payment |
| 6 | **Manufacturing** | Your doors are being manufactured |
| 7 | **Final Payment** | Waiting for your 50% final payment |
| 8 | **Shipping** | Your order is in transit |
| 9 | **Delivered** | Your doors have arrived |

Completed stages show green checkmarks. Your current stage is highlighted in blue. Future stages appear in gray.

Below the progress bar, five tabs let you navigate between different sections of your portal.

---

### Quote Tab

The **Quote** tab is your main reference for everything about your order.

**Door Preview**
An interactive animation at the top shows how your door system operates — panels slide, fold, or stack just like the real thing.

**Specifications**
A detailed grid lists every specification:
- Door Type, Material, Overall Size
- Number of Panels, Per-Panel Width, Panel Layout
- Opening Direction, Swing Direction
- Frame Color, Interior Color (if different)
- Glass Type, Hardware Finish
- System Type (if applicable)

**Pricing**
A full pricing breakdown showing:
- Per-item base price calculations (sq ft × rate)
- Glass modifier adjustments
- Subtotal, Installation, Delivery, Tax
- **Grand Total**

**Delivery Address**
An editable form where you can enter or update your delivery address:
- Street Address *(required)*
- Apt / Suite / Unit *(optional)*
- City, State, ZIP *(required)*

Click **"Save Address"** after entering your information. You'll see a confirmation when it's saved.

---

### Photos Tab

The **Photos** tab lets you upload site photos to help our team understand your space and plan the installation.

**How to Upload**
- **Drag and drop** files into the upload area, or **click to browse** your device
- You'll see upload progress and a checkmark when each file completes

**File Requirements**
| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Accepted formats | JPEG, PNG, WebP, AVIF |
| Max file size | 10 MB per image |
| Max photos | 10 per category (Interior / Exterior) |

**Photo Categories**
Photos are organized into two sections:
- **Interior** — Photos from inside the room where the doors will be installed
- **Exterior** — Photos from outside showing the opening or installation area

Each section shows a count (e.g., "Interior (2/10)") so you know how many you've uploaded.

**Managing Photos**
- Hover over any photo to see a **delete button (X)** if you need to remove it
- Photos are displayed as thumbnails in a grid

> **Tip:** Upload clear, well-lit photos that show the full opening area, surrounding walls, floor, and ceiling. This helps our team prepare accurate approval drawings and installation plans.

### Approval Drawing Tab

The **Approval Drawing** tab is where you request, review, and sign off on the detailed technical drawing of your door configuration before manufacturing begins.

**Stage 1: Request a Drawing**
When you first visit this tab, you'll see a prompt:

> *"Ready to move forward? Request an approval drawing so our team can prepare a detailed diagram of your door configuration for your review and sign-off."*

Click **"Request Approval Drawing"** to submit your request. Our team will be notified.

**Stage 2: Waiting for Your Drawing**
After requesting, you'll see:

> *"We've received your request! Our team is preparing your approval drawing. You'll receive an email notification when it's ready for review."*

**Stage 3: Review and Sign**
When your drawing is ready, you'll see:
- An information banner asking you to review and sign
- An **interactive door animation** showing your exact configuration
- A **specifications grid** with dimensions, panel count, slide direction, swing direction, system type, and configuration details
- Any **additional notes** from our team
- A **"Download PDF"** button to save a copy

When you're satisfied with the drawing, click **"Sign Approval Drawing"** to open the signature section:
1. Enter your **full name** in the text field
2. **Draw your signature** on the signature pad (works with mouse on desktop, touch on mobile/tablet)
3. Use **"Clear signature"** if you need to start over
4. Click **"Confirm & Sign"**

**Stage 4: Signed Confirmation**
After signing, you'll see a green checkmark with:

> *"Signed by [Your Name] on [Date]"*

A **"Download PDF"** button lets you save the signed approval drawing for your records.

> **Important:** Signing the approval drawing confirms that the specifications are correct and authorizes manufacturing to begin.

## 5. Contract & Payments

### Signing the Contract

After our team approves your quote, you'll receive an email with the subject:

> **Quote QT-XXXX-XXX Approved — Sign Your Contract | Scenic Doors**

Click the link to open the **Service Agreement & Contract** page. You'll see:

1. **A progress stepper** showing: Quote ✓ → **Contract** → Payment → Complete
2. **Project Specifications** — Your door animation, door type, material, size, panels, layout, colors, glass, hardware, and delivery details
3. **Payment Terms** — Total project cost and the 50% advance payment amount
4. **Terms & Conditions** covering:
   - Scenic Doors agrees to supply and install per the specifications
   - Timeline communicated upon receipt of advance payment
   - Specification changes may result in additional charges
   - Materials come with manufacturer warranties; installation warranted for 5 years
   - Cancellation within 72 hours of signing is eligible for a full refund of the advance payment

5. **Signature Section**
   - Enter your **full name**
   - **Draw your signature** on the pad
   - Read the disclaimer: *"By signing this document, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined above and authorize Scenic Doors to proceed with the project."*
   - Click **"Sign & Submit Contract"**

After signing, you'll be redirected to the invoice page for your 50% advance payment.

### Payment Schedule

|---------|--------|------|-------------------|
| **50% Advance** | Half of total | After signing contract | Manufacturing begins |

## 7. FAQ & Tips

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take to get a quote?**
A: If you use the "I know exactly what I want" path, you'll receive a detailed quote by email almost immediately. For the "General idea" and "Browse" paths, a specialist will follow up to prepare your estimate.

**Q: Can I change my quote after submitting?**
A: Yes — contact our team and they can update your quote with new specifications. You'll receive an updated version via email.

**Q: What if I'm not sure about my measurements?**
A: Choose the **"I have a general idea"** path and select "Not sure" for the size question. Our team will help you determine the right dimensions during the consultation.

**Q: How long does manufacturing take?**
A: Manufacturing timelines vary by product and configuration. Your estimated completion date will be shown in the Tracking tab once manufacturing begins.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Cancellation within 72 hours of signing the contract is eligible for a full refund of the advance payment. After that period, contact us to discuss options.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept payments through Square. You'll receive an invoice with a link to pay online.

**Q: What does the rough opening size mean?**
A: The rough opening is the framed opening in your wall where the door will be installed. It's typically 1″ wider and 1″ taller than the door itself. This measurement is shown in your quote details.

### Photo Tips

When uploading site photos to the Photos tab, keep these tips in mind:

- **Take photos in good lighting** — natural daylight works best
- **Show the full opening** — step back far enough to capture the entire area where the door will go
- **Include surrounding context** — walls, floor, ceiling, and any obstacles nearby
- **Upload both interior and exterior views** — this helps our team plan from both sides
- **Keep files under 10 MB** — if your photos are too large, resize them before uploading
- **Accepted formats:** JPEG, PNG, WebP, or AVIF
